About Me

Hi there! I'm Vishal, a creative problem-solver who thrives at the intersection of technology, engineering, innovation and art. For me, innovation isn't just about creating new things, it's about creating them with beauty and purpose. I believe artistic sensibilities are essential when crafting user experiences, engineering solutions that are elegant as well as effective, and developing technologies that inspire and solve real-world problems. This interdisciplinary approach fuels my passion for bringing ideas to life in a way that is both impactful and inspiring.

My expertise lies in Software engineering, Web Development, Data Science and Analytics & User Interface Design. I'm constantly seeking ways to leverage these skills to tackle complex challenges and make a positive social impact.
